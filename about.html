About Amrutvahini College of Engineering

History

Amrutvahini College of Engineering (AVCOE) was established in 1983 with a vision to provide quality technical education and foster innovation in engineering. Over the years, the college has grown into a premier institution known for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art facilities, and commitment to producing skilled engineers who contribute to society.

The college is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by AICTE. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, emphasizing practical learning and research.
